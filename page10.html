Ok so it's been a while

I Managed to make a few things but I feel the need to share this one that I'm proud of.

Behold the Rapid Racking Hook!

Well it just a hook that the pegs on the back of it but its quite affective for what I needed. The idea was to hang some sound dampening headphones which is very affective.

This is designed the shelving holes for rapid rackingwhich is designed to be like key holes.

It fits a little loosely but that is just in case it need to move to another location

With using Cura slicer made sure that the wall layer's set to 5 and infill wall's to 2 just to have some strength..... ok a lot of strength but I'm very much aware that the weak points are the pegs on the back that hook on the shelving.

Also you might note that there is a block on the back where the pegs are. That is some support which is connected by a very small 1mm point just to get it over to Cura without issue.

I did try to use the supports in Cura but it would not print well at all and the pegs needed to fit correctly within the racking

After the first time printing it I saw that it was warping at the corners which is why the mouse ears on those points in the picture.

Honestly its quite affective. its been there for about 3 weeks now and no issues what so ever.

Now to figure out what else to print.
