Fusion 360 is very Powerful!

I'm not kidding. In the last few weeks of using it I have managed to make over 20 Projects. Made some for family, some for friends and some to replace a temporary fix to a more permanent one (aka replacing two small bits of wood under sky Q box with a proper stand) most of the ideas have been from other people and I honestly didn't think I would adapt to it so quickly

I am actually enjoying this I am tempted to post some of my designs onto thangs.com and show them off. They are simple but for what they are they quite affective.

The Sky Q Box stand for example is just a platform with legs but in fusion 360 I used about 4-5 tools in making it

The size of it is quite big too and it took up the whole build plate of 220 X 220. I am happy with the end result some much so I'm grinning ear to ear.

On a side note I have decided to edit the project page a little bit mainly so you as someone looking at mu site can find what types of projects you are looking for. its just a simple page that lets you choose either 3d printing or web projects.

I'm just thinking that making it less confusing of what type of project is what. This page hasn't been made yet but im sure I can wip up something relatively quickly.
